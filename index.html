About the project

Overall Goal

The goal of this project is not to make a game, exactly, I want to make a foundation for a game. I want to have a working combat beta to show off to raise funding so that I can take the base we have and make an epic story with really entertaining gameplay. I want to be able to show people This game can be made and that they are not throwing away their money on something that may never come to fruition.

Goals of the project

The goal of the project is to build a working combat systems and AI as well as establish an art style for a universe while at the same time making a small game with a large amount of replayability.

Personal Goals

Most of all I want this project to be a learning experience, I haven’t even started yet and I learned how to build a website from pure HTML and CSS3. I want to further my understanding of unity and c# while gaining a deeper understanding of programing in general. I hope this project helps me to gain new colleagues and a better understanding of the artistic side of the industry. I am hoping that this is a learning experience for everyone involved in the project, I think it will give everyone a chance to gain experience working in groups as well as further understand the publishing process.
